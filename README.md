## 🎯 Key Takeaway

> Knowing *how* to build is not enough — understanding *why it works*, *how it scales*, and *how users interact with it* is what differentiates strong frontend engineers.

---

## 📌 How to Use This Roadmap

- Pick 1–2 components daily and implement from scratch  
- Focus on **edge cases + accessibility + performance**  
- Practice explaining your approach out loud (interview simulation)  
- Revisit and optimize your older implementations  

---

## ⭐ Bonus Tip

During interviews:
- Always clarify requirements  
- Think in terms of **reusability & scalability**  
- Communicate trade-offs clearly
